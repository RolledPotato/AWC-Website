Rationale behind the Logo of AWC IIT Ropar

The logo represents the Animal Welfare Committee of IIT Ropar, symbolizing the organization's commitment to promoting the well-being of animals. The focal point of the logo is a paw print, which is a universally recognized symbol of animal welfare. The paw print is stylized to also resemble a caring hand, signifying the human element of the organization's mission. The top side of the paw print is shaped to resemble people, emphasizing the connection and interdependence between humans and animals. This unique and thought-provoking design effectively communicates the organization's message and values, making it an appropriate and memorable emblem for the Animal Welfare Committee of IIT Ropar.
